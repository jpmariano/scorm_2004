Course Information

This course is designed to be interactive and will prepare you for the accompanying assessment. You must complete the entire course before you can take the assessment. To allow you to go through the course at your own pace, narration is only used as needed, for scenarios and examples, and will not be found on most course pages.

Course Requirements

The National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) and the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) have requested that the following statement be placed as a reminder in air traffic controller training courses where phraseology is discussed.

Air traffic controllers are required to use standard International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) and FAA phraseology (excluding conditional phraseology) and to speak at reasonable rates when communicating with all flight crews, especially those whose primary language is not English. Use clear and concise communications at all times.

To view FAA JO 7110.65 at any time, select the "Resources" button.

This course contains replays of actual events.  In some cases, nonstandard phraseology is used.  However, controllers are reminded that standard phraseology must be used at all times.
