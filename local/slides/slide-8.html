Hazards

Failure to clarify similar sounding call signs creates major hazards for the safety of the NAS.

These hazards include:

  • Aircraft Collisions
  • Runway Incursions
  • LoSS
  • Pilot Deviation

Wrong Aircraft Acknowledgement

A pilot may execute a clearance intended for another aircraft.

To mitigate the risk, do not abbreviate:

  • Similar sounding aircraft identifications
  • Identifications of air carriers
  • Other civil aircraft having an FAA authorized call sign

Never abbreviate similar sounding call signs.
