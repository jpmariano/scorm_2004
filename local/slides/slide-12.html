Similar Sounding Call Signs

Best Practices

Add destination to clarify call sign if necessary.

"DELTA EIGHT FORTY FOUR TO PORTLAND, TAXI TO RWY 35L."

When handing off, ensure receiving controller knows to expect aircraft with similar sounding call signs on their frequency.

"DELTA FORTY TWO AND DELTA EIGHT FORTY TWO ARE BACK TO BACK, READY FOR DEPARTURE."

Be vigilant; listen carefully to readbacks and listen for similar sounding call signs.
