Course Glossary

Acronym Meaning
LoSS Loss of Standard Separation
ICAO International Civil Aviation Organization
IATA International Air Transport Association

Loss of Standard Separation (LoSS) A loss of separation between aircraft occurs whenever specified separation minima in controlled airspace are breached. Minimum separation standards for airspace are specified by Air Traffic Service authorities, based on ICAO standards.

Runway Incursion Any occurrence at an aerodrome involving the incorrect presence of an aircraft, vehicle or person on the protected area of a surface designated for the landing and takeoff of aircraft.
